# 🔽 Lead Funnel Analysis

Queries that track leads across every stage of the AI qualification
pipeline — from first contact to appointment scheduled.

Use these to answer questions like:
- How many leads came in this week, and from which channels?
- At which stage are the most leads dropping off?
- Which sources produce the highest-intent leads?
- How is the intent score distributed across the pipeline?

---

## Queries in This Collection

### 1. Weekly Lead Volume by Source
Counts incoming leads grouped by source channel and week.
Identifies which acquisition channels are performing best.

### 2. Lead Status Distribution
Shows a snapshot of how leads are distributed across
HOT, WARM, COLD, and URGENT classifications right now.

### 3. Average Intent Score by Source
Compares the average AI-assigned score per source channel —
reveals which channels bring in the highest-quality leads.

### 4. Funnel Drop-off Analysis
Tracks how many leads enter the pipeline versus how many
reach each subsequent stage, identifying the biggest gaps.

### 5. Off-Hours Lead Capture Rate
Measures how many leads arrived outside business hours
and what percentage were successfully captured by automation.
