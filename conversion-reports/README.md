# 📈 Conversion Reports

Queries that translate pipeline data into business outcomes —
the numbers that matter to decision-makers and clients.

Use these to answer questions like:
- What is the overall lead-to-appointment conversion rate?
- How does this month compare to last month?
- Which lead source produces the highest conversion rate?
- What is the estimated revenue impact of the automation?

---

## Queries in This Collection

### 1. Overall Conversion Rate (Lead to Appointment)
The primary business metric — what percentage of leads
that enter the pipeline end up scheduling an appointment.

### 2. Conversion Rate by Lead Source
Breaks down conversion performance by acquisition channel,
identifying which sources produce the most valuable leads.

### 3. Month-over-Month Pipeline Comparison
Compares key pipeline metrics between the current and
previous month — volume, conversion rate, and throughput.

### 4. HOT Lead Conversion Rate
Specifically tracks how well high-intent leads (score 8+)
are being converted — a critical metric for validating
the AI qualification model's accuracy.

### 5. Revenue Impact Estimate
Estimates the revenue contribution of automation by
calculating appointments recovered from off-hours leads
that would otherwise have been lost.
